# Domain Suspension Guidelines

## Section 4.2: Reasons for Suspension
Domains may be suspended due to:
- Missing or invalid WHOIS contact information
- Abuse complaints (e.g., phishing, malware distribution)
- Payment or renewal failures beyond the grace period

## Section 4.3: Reactivation
To reactivate a suspended domain:
1. Update WHOIS records with valid contact details.
2. Resolve outstanding abuse complaints by replying to the abuse team.
3. If related to billing, complete payment and contact billing support.

## Section 4.4: Notifications
We send notices to the registrant email. In rare cases, emails may be filtered or bounce.
